## Cloud Native Vietnam

We are a community of cloud native enthusiasts in Vietnam, passionate about Kubernetes, containers, service mesh, observability, and the broader cloud native ecosystem.

### Our Mission

- **Share knowledge** about cloud native technologies through blog posts, meetups, and workshops
- **Build community** by connecting engineers, developers, and architects across Vietnam
- **Contribute** to open source cloud native projects

### Get Involved

- Join our community discussions
- Contribute a blog post
- Attend our meetups
